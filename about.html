Are You Planning to Put Ads on this Blog?

I am indeed. I do enjoy writing blog posts that inspire readers to not only use the information in their own lifestyle, but come back for more information on a whole range of topics. However, I'd like to be able to monetise my work, so it motivates me to create more blog posts since I'm earning real income for the work I do (I know, this sounds rather cliche).
